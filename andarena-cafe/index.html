Our Philosophy

The Cabin in the City

Andarena was born from a desire to bring the comforting warmth of a rustic cabin to the energetic heart of Riyadh. We believe a coffeehouse should be more than just a quick stop; it should be a home away from home.

With exposed brick walls, rich dark wood details, glowing filament lights, and vintage Chesterfield leather sofas, every corner of Andarena is designed to wrap you in comfort. Whether you are catching up with friends, diving into a good book, or sealing a business proposal, you'll find the perfect, quiet atmosphere here.
